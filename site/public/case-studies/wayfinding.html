Wayfinding

Lead UX Designer — Fall 2025 — 3 interviews, 10 contextual inquiries

Designed a mobile navigation app based on a hybrid indoor navigation system to improve physical wayfinding of NJIT Library. Conducted interviews and contextual inquiries, performed market research, and produced prototypes and a PRD.

Key outcomes

  • Navigation time reduced by ~9.5 minutes (~70%)
  • Improved ease of navigation by 64%
